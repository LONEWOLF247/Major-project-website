About

Jungle Run is a 2D running video game developed by our group .In this game, The player controls an explorer who has obtained an ancient relic and runs from one place to another where he hasto come-across many ofhurdles and obstacles which he has to face and safe-out himself from thesituation he will be facing on the way.


The game is released for PC & Laptop where the player can safely control the games and enjoy it. The game is to defeat all the enemies in each phase.The mechanics of Jungle Run, involve only running and jumping.


Players cannot jump on enemies and squashthem, unless they were already turned on their back. The player gains points by defeating multiple enemies consecutively and can participate in a bonus round to gain more points. Enemies are defeated by kicking them over once they have been flipped on their back
